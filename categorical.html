2. The Chi-Squared Test

The Concept: Just looking at the table isn't enough. We need to know if the relationship is significant.

We use the Chi-Squared Test of Independence ($\chi^2$).

  • Null Hypothesis ($H_0$): The variables are independent (Drinking Sherry has NO effect on Mood).
  • Alternative ($H_1$): The variables are related (Sherry leads to Feisty behavior).

Syntax: chisq.test(table)
